About Me

I’m Tomi Adebisi Tijani, a board-certified Psychiatric-Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP-BC) with a Master of Science in Nursing (MSN). With years of experience in mental health care, I am dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based treatment to help individuals achieve their best mental well-being. My approach is holistic and patient-centered, ensuring that each individual receives care tailored to their unique needs, experiences, and values. I believe in building a strong therapeutic relationship founded on trust, empathy, and collaboration. Whether you’re navigating anxiety, depression, ADHD, bipolar disorder, or other mental health challenges, I am here to guide and support you with personalized treatment plans. Committed to diversity and inclusion, I strive to create a safe and welcoming space where everyone feels heard and respected. Mental health is a journey, and I am honored to walk this path with you, helping you regain balance, build resilience, and embrace a healthier, more fulfilling life.
